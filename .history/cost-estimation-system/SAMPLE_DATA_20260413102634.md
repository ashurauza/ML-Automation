## 📈 What Gets Extracted

When a PDF is uploaded, the system extracts:

✓ **Text Data**
  - Part name and material type
  - Dimensions with tolerances
  - Manufacturing processes
  - Processing times

✓ **Cost Calculations**
  - Raw material cost
  - Machining costs (CNC, drilling, etc.)
  - Labor costs
  - Overhead (15%)
  - Logistics ($2.50/unit)
  - Profit margin (25%)

✓ **Total Cost Estimation**
  - Per-unit cost
  - Extended cost for quantity
  - Detailed breakdown
